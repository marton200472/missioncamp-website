About

Mission Camp is InterCP International's annual 3-day frontier missions conference that will awaken believers to radically obey our Lord and His Great Commission.

Through messages, testimonies, worship, and prayer, participants will be challenged to live with a global Great Commission mindset. As we learn about the context of the times and the urgency of the gospel, may we witness the greatest revival of Europe and prepare for Jesus' return!
